About Me
I am Iron Man...?

Hello there, my name is Sam and I am a student, future engineer, and prop making/electrical hobbist based in the United States. Most of my projects are based on my favorite movies/TV shows such as Star Wars or the Arrowverse, video games such as Halo, or even celebrity figures such as Daft Punk. My favorite things to do are exercise, play video games, and hang with the boys.

In the coding world, I'm decent at HTML and CSS (I mean, I made this website...), with a slight understanding of JS. I can read other languages such as Python and understand what's happening to a degree, however don't have enough knowledge to create something.

During the Coronavirus quarantine, I was browsing Reddit and saw a figurine of Captain Rex and Commander Cody on someone's wall. I thought to myself, "How hard would it be to get/make that?" A couple months later, and lots of time and money spent... I 3D print props and work on basic circuits (such as keyboards). Taking inspiration from a friend, I then made this website to document my progress and failures.
